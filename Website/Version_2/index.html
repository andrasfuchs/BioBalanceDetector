What We Do

Our goal is to find a way to show the weak electromagnetic fields around every living being (including plants, animals and humans) and display it in a heat-map like hyper-spectral image.

Our prototypes are data acquisition systems that detect, measure, display and analyze the electromagnetic energy field around living beings and indicate any potential health imbalance.
